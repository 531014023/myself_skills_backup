## 芒格与巴菲特的关系

> *"During his and Warren Buffett's reign as the leaders of Berkshire, the company has returned roughly 2,000,000% on its initial value, or 20,000 to 1."*
> （在沃伦·巴菲特和他担任伯克希尔领导期间，公司初始价值回报约为2,000,000%，即20,000比1。）

> *"This was accomplished in the adult lifetime of two men simply by investing the company's capital in an increasing number of prosperous enterprises and without dangerous amounts of borrowing."*
> （这是两位成年男性一生所取得的成就，仅仅通过将公司资本投资于越来越多的繁荣企业，且没有进行危险规模的借贷。）

---

## 芒格论能力圈

> *"I think Warren and I know the edge of our competency better than other people do."*
> （我认为沃伦和我比其他人更清楚自己能力的边界。）

---

## 芒格论决策速度

> *"Neither Warren nor I are smart enough to make decisions with no time to think. We make actual decisions very rapidly, but that's because we have spent so much time preparing ourselves by quietly reading."*
> （沃伦和我都没有聪明到可以不加思考就做决定。我们实际做决定很快，那是因为我们花了大量时间通过安静阅读来做好准备。）

---

## 芒格论风险

> *"We're just not interested in taking a substantial chance of taking a lot of very decent people back to 'Go' so we can have one more zero on our net worth."*
> （我们不想为了净资产后面多一个零，就拿大量非常正派的人冒险让他们回到起点。）

> *"It takes character to sit with all that cash and do nothing. I didn't get to where I am by going after mediocre opportunities."*
> （手握大量现金什么都不做需要品格。我追随地平庸的机会是走不到今天的。）

---

## 芒格评巴菲特特质

- **专注**
- **记忆力**
- **工作热情**
